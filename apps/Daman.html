The Perfect Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Coastal Sunsets

Arrive and check into your seaside stay. Spend the early afternoon relaxing at Devka Beach. As the sun softens, explore the historical St. Jerome Fort and the beautifully preserved Church of Our Lady of Manso. Wind down the evening exploring the Daman Ganga Tourist Complex and end with a stunning sunset at Jampore Beach.

Day 2: Heritage & Adventure

Start your day diving into history at the grand Moti Daman Fort and the iconic Bom Jesus Church. Take a scenic stroll to the Fort of St. Thomas and head up to the Lighthouse Viewpoint for panoramic sea views. In the afternoon, unwind at Mirasol Lake Garden before adding some thrill with water sports back at Jampore.

Day 3: Serenity & Departure

Wake up early for a tranquil Morning Beach Walk, soaking in the coastal breeze. Enjoy a hearty local breakfast before diving into local shopping at the vibrant Nani Daman Market—perfect for souvenirs! Finally, pack your beautiful memories and begin the relaxing drive back to Vadodara.
